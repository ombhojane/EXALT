How to Spark Your Child's Curiosity About Science

Written by Sir C.V.Raman on February 28, 1928

As a parent or caregiver, you want to give your child the best opportunities to learn and grow. One way to do that is to spark their curiosity about science. Science is everywhere around us, from the plants in the garden to the stars in the sky, and encouraging your child's interest in science can help them understand and appreciate the world around them. Here are some tips to help you spark your child's curiosity about science:
1. Ask Questions and Encourage Your Child to Ask Questions
2. Make Science Fun and Hands-On
3. Read Science Books and Watch Science Videos
4. Encourage Your Child to Explore Nature
5. Support Your Child's Interests and Curiosity
